M&V Events organizes activities where families, children, and event participants
return across different programs. A mobile loyalty and rewards app fits that
setting well: it gives families a way to keep savings cards with them, collect
points during activities, and redeem rewards without relying on a physical card.

The app supports family savings cards, QR-code based point collection, product
and reward browsing, ordered-product follow-up, and authenticated profile flows.
The public store listing describes the core loop: users carry their family's
savings cards, scan QR codes when participating in activities, collect points,
and redeem products through the shop.

My work focused on the mobile delivery side: React Native and Expo
implementation, TypeScript screens and components, authenticated frontend state,
GraphQL and REST API integration, QR-related flows, card and product interfaces,
profile interactions, and support for Android and iOS release work.
