Optimus is Team Solutions' ERP and business automation platform for service
companies. Public Team Solutions material positions the product around connected
operations, mobile field work, finance, HRM, document management, analytics, and
customer-facing workflows. That creates a software problem where planning,
execution, administration, and reporting all need to share the same operational
truth.

My work sits inside that product context: developing and maintaining FileMaker
features, improving existing workflows, and fixing production issues while
keeping the product usable for teams that depend on repeatable service and
project processes. The portfolio framing stays focused on engineering patterns
and product responsibility rather than customer-specific implementation detail.

A major part of the work has been modern planning systems for site planning,
project planning, and project job planning. These interfaces use FileMaker Web
Viewers with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to replace older planning screens with
clearer drag-and-drop scheduling, filtering, workload indicators, multi-job
operations, and FileMaker-to-JavaScript data exchange.

I also contributed to AI-assisted ERP workflows using the OpenAI API, including
email classification and structured processing of invoices and order
confirmations. The implementation pattern combines JSON-schema extraction,
FileMaker scripting, and human review so unstructured inputs can become useful
ERP data while the product workflow remains controlled and auditable.
