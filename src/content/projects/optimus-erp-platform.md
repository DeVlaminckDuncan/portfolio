Optimus is Team Solutions' business automation software for service companies.
The public Team Solutions website describes Optimus as a SaaS offering focused
on real-time business information and connected operational workflows, including
areas such as operations, finance, procurement and stock, HRM, administration,
document management, and related collaboration flows.

The public-safe version of this work focuses on the type of engineering involved
rather than private customer details or internal business rules. In that context,
the ERP work sits close to planning, operational records, finance-adjacent data,
and service workflows that need to stay clear across teams.

My work includes FileMaker feature development, maintenance, improvements, and
bug fixes across the ERP platform. A major part of that work has been modern
planning interfaces for site planning, project planning, and project job
planning, using FileMaker web viewers with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for clearer
drag-and-drop scheduling, filtering, load indicators, and multi-job operations.

I also contributed to practical AI-assisted ERP workflows using the OpenAI API,
including email classification and automated processing of invoices and order
confirmations into structured ERP data. That work uses JSON schema based
extraction integrated with FileMaker scripts, with the public framing kept to
the system pattern rather than confidential process detail.
